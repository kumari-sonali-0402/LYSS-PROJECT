About Our Mess Management System

Welcome to GEC Vaishali mess management system! This platform is designed to help students and mess administrators manage meals, attendance, billing, and feedback seamlessly.

Our goal is to reduce paperwork, streamline operations, and ensure transparency in the mess process. The system is user-friendly and responsive, working well across devices.

Daily Meals

View and manage daily menus along with nutritional information.

Attendance Tracking

Check-in daily and maintain accurate attendance records.

Billing

Auto-generate monthly bills based on meal attendance.

Feedback

Share suggestions or complaints directly with the management.
